FAQ

Questions You Might Have

Do I need to clean before you come?

No. Please don't feel like you need to prepare your home before asking for support. Spruce & Spirit Home Care is built on a no-judgement approach.

Do you offer once-off cleans?

Yes. Once-off support may be available depending on your needs and current availability.

Do you offer regular visits?

Yes. Ongoing support can be arranged for clients who benefit from regular help at home.

Do you help with organising and decluttering?

Yes. Support can include sorting, simplifying, and creating practical systems that are easier to maintain.

Do you bring cleaning supplies?

This can be discussed during your enquiry. Some clients prefer their own products to be used, while others may need supplies provided.

What areas do you service?

Yarrabilba, QLD and surrounding suburbs. Please get in touch to confirm.

How do I book?

Send an enquiry through the contact form and I will get back to you to discuss what kind of support would be the best fit.
